Frequently Asked Questions +

Is it see-through?

Our darker colors offer 100% opacity. For lighter colors (such as Light Sand or Light Gray), there is a possibility your hair may be slightly visible depending on the lighting and your hair color.

Do I need an inner (anak tudung)?

It's optional. The fabric is designed with a non-slip grip to stay secure on its own, but it works perfectly with an inner if you prefer extra coverage or hair management.

Will I feel hot?

No. The micro-perforated fabric vents heat instantly, preventing the "heavy" feeling of trapped sweat during intense workouts.

Does it stay in place?

Yes. The 12% Spandex blend ensures the hijab moves with you and stays put without the need for pins.

Is it good for sensitive skin?

Yes. Our quick-dry tech prevents salt buildup, which is the primary cause of neck itch and scalp irritation.

Does the UV protection wash out?

No. The protection is built into the actual yarn fibers, not just a coating, so it stays effective for the life of the garment.
